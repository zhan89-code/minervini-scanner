# Quick Screening Checklist

Use this as a fast pass/fail sheet when screening a batch of tickers.
For deeper explanation of any line item, see the main SKILL.md.

## Trend Template (need all 8)
- [ ] Price > 150-day MA and > 200-day MA
- [ ] 150-day MA > 200-day MA
- [ ] 200-day MA rising for 1+ month (ideally 4-5+)
- [ ] 50-day MA > 150-day MA and > 200-day MA
- [ ] Price > 50-day MA
- [ ] Price >= 30% above 52-week low
- [ ] Price within 25% of 52-week high
- [ ] Relative strength vs. market strong (RS rating 70+, ideally 80s-90s)

## Fundamentals
- [ ] EPS growth 20-25%+ YoY in most recent quarter(s), ideally accelerating
- [ ] Revenue growth present alongside EPS growth (not just cost-cutting)
- [ ] Margins flat or expanding
- [ ] Identifiable catalyst (product, management, regulatory, industry trend)
- [ ] Industry group itself showing relative strength

## Setup / Entry (VCP)
- [ ] Base shows 2-6 contractions, each shallower than the last
- [ ] Volume contracts on the final, tightest pullback
- [ ] Clear pivot point identified
- [ ] Breakout (if any) on above-average volume

## Risk plan (fill in before any hypothetical trade)
- Entry price: ___
- Stop-loss price / % risk: ___
- Target reward-to-risk ratio: 2:1 to 3:1
- Position size relative to portfolio: ___ (concentrated: ~4-12 positions typical)

## Verdict shorthand
- **Stage 2 + all 8 Trend Template + strong fundamentals + clean VCP** → high-quality candidate, watch for pivot breakout
- **Stage 2 + Trend Template pass, weak/unclear fundamentals** → technically fine, needs a story
- **Any Trend Template fail** → not a current SEPA candidate, regardless of fundamentals
- **Stage 3 or 4** → avoid / exit, not an entry candidate
